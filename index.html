The power of overthinking

Throughout the years I was unsure of what I wanted to be and do in life.

From learning new skillsets and experiences I came to a realization that I inspired to have a diverse skill set. From learning coding, SCRUM, and EXCEL I felt accomplished.

I always self doubted myself and would find myself overthinking if I was able to achieve a corporate job.

Even with driving, I felt that I was different and that I would never get over my fears with driving.

Even playing tennis, I thought I would never be a decent player because I felt that it was difficult to just believe in myself.

I felt that nothing I did would be benefitial because of my thoughts and assumption about myself.

But that was before. Now I feel that I am just making up scenarios in my head that is gatekeeping myself from success.

I want to own a company and have a diverse skillset.

You just need to believe in yourself.
